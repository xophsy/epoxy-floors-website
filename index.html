Our Install Process

  1. Day 0

    Consult & Design

    Site visit, moisture reading, color/system selection, and final quote.

  2. Day 1

    Prep & Repair

    Diamond grind or shot-blast. Crack chase and patch. Dustless cleanup.

  3. Day 2

    Prime & Build

    Moisture barrier primer and body coat. Decorative system applied.

  4. Day 3

    Seal & Turnover

    UV-stable topcoat. Light foot traffic in 24h; vehicles in ~72h.

FAQ

How long does installation take? ▾

Most garages take 1–2 days. Light foot traffic in 24 hours; vehicles after ~72 hours depending on system and weather.

Will it yellow in the sun? ▾

We use UV-stable topcoats and recommend quartz/flake systems in areas with strong UV exposure.

What’s the warranty? ▾

Lifetime warranty on residential against peeling and delamination under normal use. Commercial warranties available by system.
